What is a supercar?

A supercar is a car that is street-legal and that has high performance. Most supercars top speeds of 200 mph! Supercars are considered to be the most pristine and top of supercars. Some supercars are built to look cool, such as the McLaren. Others can be luxurious, such as the Rolls-Royce. Some cars are bult for a adrenaline rushing ride. Some cars are even meant for speed, such as the Koenigsegg.

  2. Costly and luxurious
  3. Durable
  4. Fast and high tech
